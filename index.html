Introduction

As first generation immigrants from the Philippines, my parents had to work extra hard to humbly live in Canada. What's amazing is that they managed to give our family the opportunities to travel all around the world over the years. As their daughter, I will forever be grateful to all the sights they have allowed me to see.

Despite loving everywhere we went, these are my favourite places where I would love to visit once again. All images belong to me.
